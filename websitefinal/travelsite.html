Why should somebody visit Goblin Valley?

  • There are many nooks and crannies to explore
  • Many activities like hiking, camping, and mountain biking are available
  • The scenery is remarkable. The colorful rocks and formations are unique
  • Rangers offer special edcucational events like the Scorpion Hunt and the Goblin Astronomy Fest. There is so much you can learn!
