Something I like: Activity

One thing I really like is activity. Whether I'm moving my body or using my mind, I love things that require some effort. I find a great sense of satisfaction when I complete an activity, and the process itself is entertaining. It brings me a lot of joy and I'm grateful I've discovered my passion since boredom used to lead me to harmful habits. I always need to be doing something, even if that thing is meditating, and using this need to my advantage has allowed me to develop many different talents. I've found that the more I use my brain and body, the stronger I've become. It's a very welcome consequence of something I like!
